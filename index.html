What We Do

We offer a comprehensive integrative counselling service to the local north coast area. We are integrative counsellors and practice using a number of disciplines including person-centered/humanism, cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) and psychodynamic therapy. We offer you a safe and secure place to focus on yourself, and assist you in making sense of why you are feeling the way you do.

Castle Counselling Services

At Castle Counselling services we offer you a space to talk about your issues and to focus on yourself and also to develop awareness of your experiences and assist you in making sense of why you feel they way you do.

This safe and secure space with our service will give you the opportunity to work with a qualified therapist who will offer you a confidential, secure, non-judgemental therapeutic relationship which will assist you in working through your issues to bring about possible change.
